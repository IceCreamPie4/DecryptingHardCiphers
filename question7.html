Playfair Cipher

What it is

It was invented by Charles Wheatstone in 1854. The Playfair Cipher operates on pairs of letters rather than individual letters, making it more resistant to frequency analysis attacks. It employs a square grid, known as a key square, to determine the substitution of letters in plaintext.
